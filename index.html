Description

MSI PRO MP241X 23.8" FHD Monitor

The MSI PRO MP241X is a 23.8" 16:9 75 Hz VA Monitor with a ratio of 16:9. This monitor has a Full HD 1920 x 1080 resolution and a 75 Hz refresh rate, which is meant to decrease motion blur when scrolling through documents and watching videos. Furthermore, anti-flicker and blue-light-reduction technologies aid in decreasing eyestrain when viewing the display. The small bezels also serve to keep you focused on the image and make it perfect for multi-monitor setups. To get started, just connect the MP241X to your compatible devices via the HDMI 1.4 and VGA inputs.

Wide Viewing Angle

The MSI PRO MP241X provides a wide vertical and horizontal viewing angle of 178 degrees. It immediately improves the screen color and brightness to ensure that you appreciate every detail to the fullest and that you have consistent picture quality from every angle.

VESA Mountable

The MSI PRO MP241X has a VESA Mountable design that allows you to hang your screen on the wall anyplace in your home and can also be mounted with the MSI MT81. It allows you to change the tilt of the screen to your liking. Alternatively, you can use the 75 x 75mm VESA mount to attach the monitor to a wall, swivel arm, or multi-monitor stand.

Flexible Connectivity

The MSI PRO MP241X Monitor has a VGA input and an HDMI 1.4 port. You can maintain flexibility for your various devices by using dual sources. It also includes a Kensington Lock for extra security.
